My Love for Hockey

Why Hockey Matters to Me

Hockey has always been my favourite sport. I enjoy the fast pace of the game, the teamwork it requires, and the excitement of scoring a goal. It keeps me active and helps me build strong connections with my teammates. I have met so many cool people while playing hockey. I have one friend from Latvia and another from Russia. I know people almost everywhere I go because of hockey. I have also learned many life skills and leadership skills that I use every day. Hockey has taught me many life lessons, and it has also helped me grow and mature quickly. I am very grateful for everything that hockey has given me.
